About Wellness Architecture Framework

A structured approach to building and sustaining holistic personal wellness.

What is the Wellness Architecture Framework?

Wellness Architecture Framework is a structured approach designed to help individuals make informed choices in building and sustaining holistic personal wellness. Through this model, individuals learn strategies for building and maintaining a balanced, efficient, resilient, and sustainable approach to health across multiple wellness dimensions.

Background and Insights

The framework draws from decades of insights across health, wellness, psychology, and environmental sciences. It applies best practices to help individuals design a wellness lifestyle that aligns with their goals and values. Through the lens of wellness architecture, individuals can assess their practices across chosen pillars and receive tailored recommendations for optimizing their well-being.

Who is it For?

This framework is intended for anyone interested in fostering a balanced approach to wellness. It provides foundational questions to evaluate choices against wellness best practices, offering a consistent approach to pursuing a fulfilling, resilient, and sustainable lifestyle.
